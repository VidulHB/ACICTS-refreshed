About

ACICTS, the Ananda College Information and Communication Technology Society, has established itself as a leading force in the Sri Lankan ICT landscape. With a rich history dating back to the 1980s, ACICTS has consistently pushed the boundaries of technology and innovation.

From its humble beginnings as a computer club, ACICTS has evolved into a dynamic organization that fosters a culture of creativity, problem-solving, and technological excellence. Through a wide range of initiatives, including workshops, competitions, and research projects, ACICTS empowers students to explore the latest advancements in technology and develop the skills needed to succeed in the digital age.

ACICTS has achieved remarkable success both locally and internationally, winning numerous accolades and recognitions. The annual ICT event organized by ACICTS is a highly anticipated event that showcases the talent and innovation of young minds.

As ACICTS continues to thrive, it remains committed to its mission of inspiring and empowering the next generation of tech leaders.
